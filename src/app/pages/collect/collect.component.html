Bjarne Melgaard
My Trip

My Trip has it all: a journey to the farthest reaches of the dark web, DMT hallucinations and existential crises. Explore the techno-underground with the artist’s weird and wacky crew. Melgaard raises the contradictory view that if we are to continue to exist, humans will have to cease to procreate, as a result of the carbon impact of producing new human life.

The VR experience which inspires this film was shown at Sundance and is accompanied by a soundtrack created specifically for My Trip by Melgaard’s long-time collaborator Romina Cohn. Watch the full trailer above.

Bjarne Melgaard is a Norwegian artist, curator, writer, film producer and fashion designer. He has participated in the Venice (2011), Lyon (2013) and Whitney (2014) Biennials, as well exhibited extensively in major museums and institutions world-wide.
